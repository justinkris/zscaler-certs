HTTP GET Order of Operations — Memory Palace
Image Prompts (13 Stages)
============================================================

STAGE 01 — Firewall
A person in a bright Fortinet-branded t-shirt wearing a Bowser-shell necklace,
floating amongst fluffy clouds, approaching a massive wall of roaring fire with
two doors — one heavily draped in spider webs, one plain. Cinematic, slightly
cartoonish, dramatic lighting.

STAGE 02 — Web Module Entry
A person pushing through an ornate doorway completely covered in thick,
dusty spider webs. Beyond the doorway, glowing server racks and proxy
machinery can be glimpsed. Moody, slightly eerie atmosphere.

STAGE 03 — SSL Inspection
A large cartoon snake with a mischievous grin, slithering up to the protagonist
and gesturing "hands up." The snake wears a tiny security guard hat. Glowing
padlocks floating in the air are being unlocked and opening up. Playful,
illustrative style.

STAGE 04 — Security Exceptions
A snake in a bouncer's outfit holding a velvet rope next to a neon "VIP" sign.
He's waving the protagonist away with a dismissive gesture. A shorter but
fancier-dressed crowd queues behind the velvet rope. Nightclub aesthetic, punchy colors.

STAGE 05 — Malicious URLs
Santa Claus at a checkpoint kiosk in a cloud kingdom, holding a massive scrolling
parchment labeled "Naughty List." He's giving the protagonist a thumbs up and
laughing warmly. Whimsical, festive style with floating cloud platforms.

STAGE 06 — Cloud App Control
A giant cumulus cloud with a friendly, slowly-forming face hovering over a
sky platform, offering a cold can of pale ale to the protagonist. The cloud
has kind, wise eyes and puffy cheeks. Dreamlike, soft golden-hour lighting.

STAGE 07 — URL Filtering
A smaller, younger cloud with a face next to the big cloud from the previous
scene — wearing comically oversized nerd glasses and holding a clipboard with
category labels. He's inspecting a URL floating in midair. Same dreamy sky
aesthetic as before.

STAGE 08 — Browser Control
A Bowser-shell necklace hovering mid-air, mechanically unfolding and transforming
like a Transformer, revealing a glowing signature panel with Mario's handwritten
autograph. Scanning beams of light surrounding it. High-energy, dynamic angle.

STAGE 09 — Country-Based Restrictions
A tennis player being stopped by a reporter with a microphone at the edge of a
grand tennis court. The player has his hands raised mid-joke, wearing a sheepish
grin, with a pair of handcuffs comically dangling from one wrist. A globe-map
backdrop with country flags floating around. Aussie Open atmosphere.

STAGE 10 — IPS Signatures
A tennis player at the Australian Open surrounded by a crowd of enthusiastic
fans thrusting notebooks, tennis balls, and t-shirts forward for autographs.
The player is signing rapidly with a Sharpie, overwhelmed but smiling.
Stadium lights and blue courts in the background.

STAGE 11 — Suspicious Content Protection
A single ominous book glowing bright red amongst a stack of normal books being
handed to a tennis player for autographs. The open page displays the word "RISK"
in shimmering, ethereal light. The player is recoiling slightly. Mysterious,
slightly supernatural vibe.

STAGE 12 — P2P Control
Security guards forming a barrier around a tennis player, with a glowing
purple magical cage (Veigar-style Event Horizon) surrounding the pressing
crowd. The cage has dark-sorcerer energy crackling around it. Fantasy-meets-reality
overlay style.

STAGE 13 — Bandwidth Control
An exhausted tennis player slumped on a bench after a long day, a visible
battery icon hovering above his head at 1% red. Scattered autograph books,
a half-empty pale-ale can, and Sharpies litter the ground around him.
End-of-day golden light, slightly melancholy tone.
